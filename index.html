About Us

Sri Sathyachandra Enterprises is a trusted FMCG and snacks distributor based in Eluru, dedicated to delivering quality products to local villages and towns. Founded under the leadership of CEO A V V Chandradhar, we aim to provide affordable and reliable FMCG and snack products at every doorstep.

We operate with a Registered GST status: 37BCMPA5229G1ZO (Regular).

Our commitment: shop to shop distribution, bilingual support, and extensive product variety.

Our Services & Highlights

  • Wide range of FMCG & snacks products.
  • Shop-to-shop reliable distribution across villages.
  • Affordable pricing targeting village markets.
  • Bilingual product information for easy understanding (English & Telugu).
